Admin Help & Guide

Quick pointers to manage products, orders, customers, content, reports, coupons, and FAQs.

Quick Start

  1. Open the sidebar and choose a section (Products, Orders, Customers, etc.).
  2. Use the search bar and filters at the top of each page to narrow results.
  3. Click action buttons like Add, Edit, Delete to manage records.

Products Catalog

Add a Product
  1. Go to Products ‚Üí click Add.
  2. Fill Name, Brand, Price, Category, Thumbnail, and Stock status.
  3. Save to create the product. It appears in the list instantly.
Edit / Delete
  1. Find the product via search or filters.
  2. Click Edit or Delete.
  3. Confirm changes in the modal.
Bulk Upload

Use the Bulk Upload button to import many products at once (UI provided).

View Product Details

Click View to open the dedicated product page with images and all fields.

Orders

Find Orders
  • Use search by order #, customer name, or email.
  • Filter by status and date range.
Update Status
  1. Open the order row to view details and items.
  2. Choose new status (pending / fulfilled / cancelled) and save.
